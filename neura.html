Neura

iOS iPadOS macOS Coming Soon

Local Intelligence with a face.

Neura is your private, on-device AI assistant for Apple Silicon. Integrate powerful AI chat, note processing, smart reminders, and content generation into your workflow, all offline.

Highlights:

  • Truly Private & Offline: 100% on-device processing. No data leaves your device.
  • Integrated AI Chat: Discuss notes, reminders, or general topics contextually.
  • Smart Reminders & Notes: Use natural language for reminders; process text & audio notes with AI.
  • Daily Digest & Discover: Personalized summaries of your day plus optional new facts & insights.
  • Customizable: Optional animated companion, gamification, and unique UI themes (Terminal, Game Boy).
  • Voice Input: Dictate chats, notes, and reminders easily.
  • Optimized: Built with SwiftUI and MLX for efficient Apple Silicon performance.
